1. Hello World

by J. Zhao

I’m apprehensive to call this a blog, or any sort of online journal. The goal isn’t to document my dailies. Rather, I hope to be able to record some of the interesting thoughts or events in my life. And for that, I think the simple name — ‘A Journal’ is appropriate.

In addition, I think that this will also be a good exercise in composition, which quite frankly, is one of my weakest skills. I rarely write when I don’t have to, but I've decided to give it a go as a hobby to help collect my thoughts. For now, let’s just call this an experiment.
